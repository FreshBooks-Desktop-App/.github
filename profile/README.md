## 🚀 Overview
FreshBooks for Windows brings cloud accounting and invoicing to the desktop, giving freelancers and small business owners a single place to handle the money side of their work. You can build and send professional invoices, record and categorize expenses, track the hours you spend on client projects, and see where your business stands at a glance. Because the data lives in the cloud, signing in to your FreshBooks account keeps your books consistent whether you open the app on your desktop or check in from another device. It is designed for people who would rather spend time on their clients than on bookkeeping.

## ✨ Features
- **Professional Invoicing** — Create branded invoices, set payment terms, and send automatic reminders so you get paid on time.
- **Expense Tracking** — Log receipts, categorize spending, and keep every business cost ready for tax time.
- **Time Tracking** — Record billable hours against clients and projects, then turn tracked time straight into an invoice.
- **Financial Reporting** — Review profit and loss, spending, and outstanding balances to understand how your business is doing.
- **Client Management** — Keep contact details, project history, and past invoices for every client in one organized place.

## ❓ FAQ
**Is FreshBooks free?**
FreshBooks is a paid product offered through several tiered plans that scale with the size of your business. A free trial is available so you can try invoicing, expense tracking, and time tracking before you choose a plan. For current plan details, consult the official FreshBooks website.

**How does FreshBooks compare to QuickBooks?**
Both FreshBooks and QuickBooks are respected accounting tools. FreshBooks is often favored by freelancers and service-based small businesses for its straightforward invoicing and time tracking, while QuickBooks offers deep accounting features that suit businesses with more complex bookkeeping. The right choice depends on how involved your accounting needs are.

**Which versions of Windows are supported?**
FreshBooks for Windows runs on modern 64-bit versions of Windows, including Windows 10 and Windows 11, with an internet connection for its cloud features.

**Is my data secure?**
FreshBooks stores your financial data in the cloud with security and access controls in place. Signing in to your account keeps your books protected, and you can review current security practices on the official FreshBooks website.
